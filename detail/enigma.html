Enigma Puzzle

A mind-bending puzzle adventure from the early 2000s
Puzzle Strategy

Enigma Puzzle is a captivating puzzle game that challenges your wit and dexterity. Released in 2007, this open-source marvel is a spiritual successor to the classic game Oxyd. Enigma Puzzle takes you on a journey through a myriad of intricate levels, each presenting a unique logic puzzle that requires both mental prowess and precise control.

Navigate through approximately 2500 landscapes, known as ‘landscapes’, which may be played in any order. Control one or more marbles with your mouse, interacting with the environment to solve puzzles, race against time, or overcome various hazards.

With its complex puzzles and realistic physics simulation, Enigma Puzzle offers an engrossing experience that stands the test of time, appealing to both puzzle enthusiasts and those who enjoy a strategic challenge.

Strategic Gameplay and Realistic Physics

The gameplay of Enigma Puzzle is a testament to the beauty of simplicity and the depth of strategy. The game's physics are grounded in realism, making every movement of the marble an exercise in precision and foresight. Each level is a new enigma, with obstacles and puzzles that will test your problem-solving skills to the limit.

Whether you're a seasoned puzzle solver or new to the genre, Enigma Puzzle is a timeless game that offers endless hours of intellectual stimulation and fun. It's a must-try for anyone who appreciates the art of puzzle design and the thrill of a challenge.
