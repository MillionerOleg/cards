Burning Fists: Force Striker

An electrifying combat title from the golden era of arcade gaming
Action Fighting

Burning Fists: Force Striker is a thrilling combat experience that plunges you into the depths of arcade-style fighting. Released as the first new fighting title for the Sega CD in over a decade, this rich and highly playable fighter contains all the excitement and hidden moves you expect in extreme, brutal combat.

Engage in the fray with a unique set of characters, each boasting a distinct set of fighting moves. Dive into two modes of play: Single Player Mode and Two-Player Battles, where you can beat your buddies to a pulp! Compatible with both controller styles, including an additional special 6-Button Controller setup.

With its rich gameplay and detailed combat mechanics, Burning Fists: Force Striker stands as a testament to the enduring appeal of the fighting genre. It's a must-experience for enthusiasts of the style and those who relish the rush of a good brawl.

Dynamic Combat and Retro Charm

The combat system in Burning Fists: Force Striker is a spectacle of strategic fighting, demanding quick reflexes and tactical prowess. Each clash is a display of powerful strikes, agile maneuvers, and dramatic special moves that will captivate any fan of the genre.

Whether you're nostalgic for the classic arcade era or simply in search of an action-packed fighting challenge, Burning Fists: Force Striker delivers an adrenaline-pumping experience that echoes the spirit of retro gaming at its finest.
