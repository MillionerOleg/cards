Cyber Racers

A futuristic racing game from the late 90s
Racing Sci-Fi

Cyber Racers is a cult classic racing game from the late 1990s that puts you in the driver's seat of futuristic vehicles as you compete in high-speed races across neon-lit cyberpunk cityscapes.

With its unique blend of fast-paced gameplay, vibrant visuals, and pumping electronic soundtrack, Cyber Racers was ahead of its time and quickly gained a dedicated following among retro gaming enthusiasts.

Choose from a variety of sleek, customizable hover cars and take on challenging tracks filled with twists, turns, and obstacles. Upgrade your ride with powerful weapons and defensive systems to gain an edge over your opponents.

Immersive Cyberpunk Racing Experience

Cyber Racers immerses you in a gritty, neon-drenched future where underground racing is the ultimate thrill. Compete against AI racers or challenge your friends in local multiplayer mode for high-stakes races that will put your driving skills to the test.

With its challenging gameplay, stunning visuals, and unforgettable soundtrack, Cyber Racers is a must-have for any fan of classic racing games or cyberpunk fiction.
